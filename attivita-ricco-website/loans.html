Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get approved for a business loan?

+

Our commitment is to process and approve your loan within 12 hours of receiving your complete application. This includes document verification, credit assessment, and final approval. Once approved, funds are disbursed immediately.

What are the minimum and maximum loan amounts?

+

We offer business loans ranging from KES 50,000 to KES 5,000,000. The exact amount you qualify for depends on your business needs, repayment capacity, and the information provided in your application.

Do I need collateral for a business loan?

+

Our loans are designed to be accessible with minimal requirements. While we don't always require traditional collateral, having a guarantor is mandatory. The specific terms depend on the loan amount and your business profile.

What interest rates do you offer?

+

Our interest rates are competitive and transparent, with no hidden fees. The exact rate depends on factors such as loan amount, term, and your credit profile. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs.

Can I apply if my business is new or unregistered?

+

Yes, we consider applications from both registered and unregistered businesses. While business registration can strengthen your application, it's not always required. We evaluate each application based on the business concept, repayment capacity, and guarantor information.
