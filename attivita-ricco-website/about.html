About Attivita Ricco Limited

Empowering Kenyan businesses with innovative financial solutions

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize business financing in Kenya, Attivita Ricco Limited has emerged as a leading financial institution dedicated to supporting the growth and success of local entrepreneurs and businesses.

We recognized the challenges that Kenyan businesses face when seeking quick and reliable financing. Traditional lending processes often involve lengthy paperwork, extended waiting periods, and complex approval procedures that can delay critical business decisions.

Our innovative approach combines cutting-edge technology with personalized service to deliver what we believe every business deserves: fast, fair, and accessible financing solutions.
